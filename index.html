Vision

We're developing a real-life video game skill tree to gamify your self-improvement journey.

With skills, items, challenges, levels, XP, badges, and more, the skill tree makes achieving your goals effortless and aims to improve mental and physical health worldwide.

Projects

Skills tree

We’ve released a huge 6000x1000px poster for you to download with every skill, item, and challenge in the skill tree completely free. We’re working on a version you can print effortlessly to mark off your skills and track your progress with a physical copy of the tree.

Discord bot

The skill tree is most easily accessible through our discord bot. You can add it to your own server in seconds and update your tasks, start, skip, and cancel skills, view your items, view your profile and XP, and get weekly reports to assess your progress and keep you on the right track.

Mobile App

Currently, in development, our mobile app allows you to easily access the skill tree from anywhere with seamless tracking, a beautiful interface, and social features to help kickstart your self-improvement journey. The app is set to be released by 2023.

Developing

Development is crucial for making our products reliable, intuitive, and easy to maintain across collaborators. Our development teams are currently working on the discord bot itself, a mobile app, our own custom API to let the bot and mobile app interface with the same database, our own website, and a specialised in-house tool for admins to edit the tree quickly and view changes visually.
